Paste prose; get XS3. Text written in the controlled language the renderer produces is inverted exactly and instantly — no model, no download. Free-form prose falls through to a neural model you can load on demand. The language itself lives at llms-language.txt.

2Neural model (flan-t5-base, on demand). For free-form prose E₀ can't invert. Constrained decoding keeps the output a valid XS3 graph. Off-distribution graph-exact ≈ 0.52, edge-F1 ≈ 0.82. It is a draft — verify before trusting.
